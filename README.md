## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎨 [Draw My Story](https://childrens-book.vercel.app) · *HopperHacks 2026*
> **🏆 Best Snowflake API Integration · Honorable Mention: Best UI/UX**

A full-stack AI platform that turns children's drawings into narrated, personalized stories. Kids upload their artwork, and the app transforms it into a beautifully illustrated, read-aloud storybook — making bedtime reading magical and accessible, especially for kids whose parents can't always be there.

**Stack:** React 18 · Node.js · Express · Google Gemini · ElevenLabs · DigitalOcean CDN · Snowflake  
**Team:** Jillian, Pravin, Christina, Anderson · [→ Repo](https://github.com/jillidc/childrens_book)

### 🔒 [LOCK-IN Timer](https://github.com/TheAndyPi/LOCK-IN-Timer)

An AI-powered Pomodoro timer built out of personal necessity during a brutal finals season. It doesn't just count down — it actively monitors your presence and focus using computer vision, and responds with psychologically-grounded motivational nudges when your attention drifts. It's a digital accountability partner, not just a clock.

**Stack:** React 19 · TypeScript · Vite · Google Gemini API · TensorFlow.js

**Stack:** Python

### 🌍 [World of Food](https://github.com/lilkahuna/World-Of-Food)

### 😮‍💨 StopSigh · CSE 300 — Technical Communication, SBU
A student productivity app with a twist: it has a living mascot whose emotional state mirrors how well you're managing your time. Miss a deadline and your companion suffers. Stay on top of things and it thrives. 

This was built collaboratively as part of a technical communication course alongside teammates Daniel, Hailey, Ying Yi, and Megan. It is not intended to be a functional app, but rather a UI mockup of what the future of how AI can transform the future of accountability tools.

**Stack:** React · TypeScript · Vite · Tailwind CSS
